The method

Three steps, no questionnaire

  1. 01

    Point it at a page

    Drop in a live URL or upload a screenshot. DesignScan captures the layout, type, color and structure as a real visitor receives it.

  2. 02

    It reads against the rubric

    Every element is checked against six calibrated criteria drawn from established usability heuristics and accessibility standards — not vibes.

  3. 03

    You get the fixes

    A scored breakdown lands with the highest-impact issues first, each paired with a concrete change your team can ship today.

The rubric

Six things we measure

Each criterion is scored independently, so you see exactly where the points leak — and where you already win.

Visual hierarchy

82

Does the eye land where it should? We check contrast, scale, spacing and grouping against the page's actual goal.

Usability

71

Clear affordances, predictable navigation, forgiving interactions — measured against Nielsen's heuristics.

Accessibility

64

Contrast ratios, focus order, target sizes and semantics, mapped to WCAG 2.2 AA where it matters most.

Consistency

88

One type scale, one spacing system, one voice. We flag the drift that quietly erodes trust.

Conversion clarity

76

Is the next action obvious? We weigh CTA prominence, friction and the path from arrival to action.

Performance signals

69

Perceived speed, layout stability and the loading experience that shapes a first impression.

sample-store.com — report.txt Overall 78/100
  • HIGH

    Primary CTA competes with three secondary buttons

    Fix: Demote secondary actions to text links so "Add to cart" is the only filled button above the fold. Expected lift in click-through clarity.

  • HIGH

    Body text contrast fails AA on the hero

    Fix: #8A8A8A on white reads 2.9:1. Darken to #595959 to clear the 4.5:1 threshold without touching the palette.

  • MED

    Inconsistent spacing between sections

    Fix: Three different vertical gaps (48 / 56 / 72px) read as accidental. Snap to an 8px scale — pick 64px and hold it.

  • LOW

    Focus state missing on the search field

    Fix: Add a 2px visible focus ring so keyboard users can see where they are.
